Kot Tra Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 6, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Kot Tra (“we”, “our”, “us”). Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application (the “App”). The App is designed to help you track finances locally on your device.

2. Information We Collect

Kot Tra does NOT collect personal information that identifies you individually (such as name, email, address, phone number, etc.). All financial data (accounts, transactions, settings) is stored locally on your device using secure local storage.

3. Local Storage Only

All user data is stored locally on your device using a local database. We do not transfer your data to servers, third parties, or remote databases. Your information stays on your device unless you choose to export or backup.

4. Backup and Export

The App provides a backup feature that allows you to export your data as a JSON file. This backup file contains your accounts, transactions, and settings. You are fully responsible for storing and protecting this backup file securely. We do not store or process your backups.

5. Ads and Premium

Free users may see advertisements in the App. Ads do not collect or share personal information beyond what is required by the ad network itself. Premium users who subscribe to the ad-free version will not see advertisements. No personal data is shared with advertisers.

6. No Third-Party Tracking

We do not use tracking or analytics tools that collect user interactions or behavior outside of the App. The App does not communicate with external analytics or advertising services to track usage.

7. Children’s Privacy

Kot Tra does not knowingly collect or solicit personal information from children under the age of 13. If you believe we collected information from a child, please contact us to request deletion.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make significant changes, we will update the last updated date at the top of this page. Your continued use of the App after we publish changes indicates your acceptance of those changes.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy policy, please contact us through the support email provided in the App Store listing.
